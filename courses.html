B.tech in Computer Science Engineering

About the course

Bachelor of Technology or BTech Computer Science is an undergraduate course of 4-years. This course is ideal for students who wish to pursue their career in the field of Computer Science as a Software Engineer or Developer in top MNCs or IT firms.


BTech Computer Science involves the study of Calculus, Algorithms, Computer Architecture, Database learning, etc, and provides a basic knowledge related to Computer Science.

Tution/course fee & Duration

  • Course level: Undergraduate
  • Course Fees: INR 80,000 to INR 1,20,000 per semester
  • Duration: 4 Years

Eligibilty criteria & Admission

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. To have scored minimum Pass Marks in JEE
  • Admission Process: Entrance Exam
  • Entrance Exams: JEE Main

Faculty

Department of Technology and sciences

Syllabus and Course Structure

  • This course is basically a platform to learn basic concepts and advanced programming of computers. It also paves ways to learn a large number of computer languages such as Java, Linux, etc.
  • The syllabus of BTech Computer Science offers students a vast knowledge of Calculus, Algorithms, Computer Architecture, Database Learning, etc.
  • On completion of this course, you will be able to become a well-known programmer.

Career-options & Oppurtunities

  • Top Recruiting Companies:TCS, HCL, Infosys, Accenture, etc.
  • Job options: Software Engineer, Testing Engineer, System Analyst and Technical Support Manager, etc
  • Average Salary Package: INR 3 - 12 Lakhs
  • Higher Studies: M.Tech Computer Science, M.Tech Information Technology, MBA, etc.


B.tech in Electrical Engineering

About the course

BTech Electrical Engineering is an undergraduate degree course in Electrical Engineering. The course has a total duration of 4 years. BTech Electrical Engineering is one of the most popular branches of Engineering.


It mainly focuses on the study of electricity and its various applications. The course also deals with the concepts of electronics and electromagnetism the important subjects that are taught to the students includePower System, Circuit Analysis, Power Electronics, Control System, etc.

Tution/course fee & Duration

  • Course level: Undergraduate
  • Average Course Fee: INR 4 Lakhs to INR 10 Lakhs
  • Duration: 4 years/8 semesters

Eligibilty criteria & Admission

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM as compulsory subjects To have scored minimum Pass Marks in JEE
  • Admission Process: BTech Entrance Exams or Merit Based
  • Entrance Exams: JEE Main

Faculty

Department of Technology and sciences

Syllabus and Course Structure

  • The course teaches the students about the functions of various electrical components. The students will get to know how to use the electric components to develop major electrical equipment.
  • he trained electrical engineers get to design computer chips, electrical motors, aircrafts, automobiles, space crafts and different other types of engines.
  • The knowledge acquired from the BTech Electrical Engineering course can be used in various fields like geoscience, acoustics, robotics, ferroelectrics, automobiles, frequency control and many more.

Career-options & Oppurtunities

  • Top Recruiting Companies: Google, Amazon, HCL, Microsoft, Infosys, TCS, Mahindra etc.
  • Job Positions: Electrical Manager, Maintenance Engineer, Professor
  • Average Salary: INR 3.6 LPA to INR 6.5 LPA
  • Higher Study Options:MTech, ME, MBA


B.tech in Mechanical Engineering

About the course

BTech Mechanical Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate course and is one of the most sought-after degree courses in engineering stream.


BTech Mechanical Engineering is the branch of engineering that involves production, designing, and operations of machines. The course objective of mechanical engineering is to prepare students for applying the principles of mechanical engineering for designing, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

Tution/course fee & Duration

  • Course level: Undergraduate
  • Course Fee: INR 4 LPA to 10 LPA
  • Duration: 4 Years

Eligibilty criteria & Admission

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. To have scored minimum Pass Marks in JEE
  • Admission Process: Entrance Exam
  • Entrance Exams: JEE Main

Faculty

Department of Technology and sciences

Syllabus and Course Structure

  • Machine Drawing and Computer Graphics, Mechanical Engineering Design, Mechanics of solids, Mechanical Vibration, Advanced Solid Mechanics, MechanicalHandling Equipment, Advanced Fluid Mechanics, Automobile Engineering,Ergonomics, etc are some of the core BTech Mechanical Engineering subjects.
  • Practical learning is integrated into the curriculum through B.Tech projects and by providing an opportunity for summer internship with national and international University and industry to solve engineering and scientific problems.

Career-options & Oppurtunities

  • Top Recruiting Companies:L&T, Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors and such others.
  • Job options: Mechanical Design Engineer, Fresher Mechanical Engineer, Solid-Edge Mechanical Design Engineer, Network Engineer, etc
  • Average Salary Package: INR 3 LPA to 7 LPA
  • Higher Studies: MTech or MBA
